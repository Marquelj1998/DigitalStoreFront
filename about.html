Why We Chose Digital Comics

We chose digital comics for their accessibility, convenience, and the ability to reach a wider audience. Digital comics allow readers to enjoy their favorite stories anytime, anywhere, without the need for physical storage.

Also because our CEO is a huge fan of digital comics!
